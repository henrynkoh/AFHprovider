## Introduction

### What is AFH Provider Matching?

AFH Provider Matching is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to connect Adult Family Home (AFH) potential homeowners with qualified providers in Centralia, WA and surrounding areas. The platform aggregates data from the Adult Family Home Council of Washington State, providing users with access to over 580 verified providers, complete with contact information, business details, and operational metrics.

### Who Should Use This Platform?

- **Potential AFH Homeowners**: Individuals or families looking to open an Adult Family Home and seeking experienced providers
- **Existing AFH Owners**: Current operators looking to expand their business or find partnership opportunities
- **Healthcare Professionals**: Medical professionals, social workers, and case managers seeking AFH providers for their clients
- **Investors**: Real estate investors or business investors interested in the AFH market
- **Researchers**: Researchers studying the AFH industry and provider distribution

### Key Benefits

- **Time Savings**: Quickly find providers without manually searching through multiple websites
- **Comprehensive Data**: Access to verified contact information and business details
- **Easy Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison of multiple providers
- **Real-time Updates**: Data is regularly updated from official sources
- **Mobile Friendly**: Access the platform from any device, anywhere

## Search Functionality

### Basic Search

**How to Perform a Basic Search:**

1. Click on the search bar at the top of the page
2. Type your search term (e.g., "Centralia", "Medicaid", "360")
3. Results update automatically as you type
4. Matching providers appear in the table below

**What Gets Searched:**

The search function looks through:
- Business names
- Provider names
- City/Area names
- Phone numbers
- Website URLs
- Additional notes/descriptions

**Search Tips:**

- **Partial Matches**: You don't need to type the full name (e.g., "Arian" will find "Arianna Care")
- **Case Insensitive**: Search is not case-sensitive ("CENTRALIA" = "centralia")
- **Multiple Words**: You can search for multiple words (e.g., "Centralia LLC")
- **Numbers**: Search for phone numbers by entering digits (e.g., "360807")

### Quick Filters

Quick filter buttons provide one-click access to common searches:

- **Centralia**: Shows all providers in Centralia, WA
- **Medicaid**: Shows providers that accept Medicaid
- **LLC**: Shows providers that are Limited Liability Companies

**How to Use Quick Filters:**

1. Click on any quick filter button
2. The search bar automatically populates with the filter term
3. Results update immediately
4. Click the X button or clear the search to return to all providers

### Advanced Search Techniques

**Searching by Phone Number:**

- Enter any part of a phone number (e.g., "360807" finds "3608074237")
- Phone numbers are stored without formatting, so search for digits only

**Searching by Business Type:**

- "LLC" - Limited Liability Companies
- "AFH" - Adult Family Homes
- "Home" - Any business with "Home" in the name
- "Care" - Any business with "Care" in the name

**Searching by Payment Type:**

- "Medicaid" - Providers accepting Medicaid
- "Private" - Private pay providers
- "Both" - Providers accepting both payment types

**Combining Search Terms:**

You can combine multiple search terms:
- "Centralia Medicaid" - Shows Medicaid providers in Centralia
- "LLC 360" - Shows LLC providers with phone numbers starting with 360

### Clearing Search Results

**Methods to Clear Search:**

1. Click the X button in the search bar
2. Delete all text in the search bar
3. Click a different quick filter button
4. Refresh the page

#### 3. Name of Provider Column

- **Purpose**: The name of the individual provider or owner
- **Format**: Provider's name
- **Sorting**: Click header to sort alphabetically
- **Note**: May be the same as business name for some providers

#### 4. Phone Number Column

- **Purpose**: Contact phone number
- **Format**: 10-digit number (e.g., "3608074237")

  - Yellow badge: "Contact via AFHC" (no direct phone available)
  - Blue link: Clickable phone number

#### 5. Website Column

- **Purpose**: Provider's website or AFHC listing
- **Format**: Website URL or "View on AFHC"
- **Features**:
  - External link icon (‚Üó)
  - Opens in new tab
  - "View on AFHC" links to official listing

#### 6. Year Started Column

- **Purpose**: Year the business was established
- **Format**: 4-digit year (e.g., "2020")
- **Sorting**: Click header to sort numerically
- **Display**: Blue badge with year
- **Note**: Shows "N/A" if year is unknown or 0

#### 7. Resident Beds Column

- **Purpose**: Number of resident beds available
- **Format**: Number followed by "Beds" (e.g., "6 Beds")
- **Sorting**: Click header to sort numerically
- **Display**: Green badge

- **Format**: Text description, may include:

## Data Management

### Understanding the Data

**Data Source:**

All provider data is collected from the Adult Family Home Council of Washington State website:
- Official source: https://adultfamilyhomecouncil.org/home-finder
- Data is scraped and updated regularly
- Phone numbers are extracted from contact modals

**Data Accuracy:**

- Data is collected from official sources
- Phone numbers are verified through contact modals
- Business information is extracted from provider listings
- Some fields may show "N/A" or "Not specified" if information is unavailable

**Data Updates:**

- Data is updated periodically through automated scraping
- New providers are added as they register
- Contact information is updated when available
- The platform shows the most current data available

### Exporting Data

**Current Limitations:**

The platform currently doesn't have built-in export functionality. However, you can:

1. **Copy from Table**: Select and copy data directly from the table
2. **Browser Print**: Use browser's print function to save as PDF
3. **Screenshot**: Take screenshots of specific providers
4. **Manual Entry**: Manually enter data into your preferred format

**Future Enhancement:**

Export functionality (CSV, PDF, Excel) is planned for future versions.

### Data Privacy

**What Data is Collected:**

- The platform does not collect personal information from users
- Provider data is publicly available information
- No cookies or tracking are used for user identification

**Data Usage:**

- Provider data is used solely for matching purposes
- No data is sold or shared with third parties
- All data remains within the platform

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Issue: Page Won't Load

**Symptoms:**
- Blank page
- Loading spinner never stops
- Error message in browser

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if JavaScript is enabled
6. Disable browser extensions temporarily

#### Issue: No Providers Displayed

**Symptoms:**
- Table shows "No providers found"
- Empty table
- "Loading..." message persists

**Solutions:**
1. Check if search filter is active (clear search)
2. Refresh the page
3. Check browser console for errors (F12)
4. Verify internet connection
5. Try accessing the API directly: `/api/providers`

#### Issue: Search Not Working

**Symptoms:**
- Typing in search bar doesn't filter results
- Results don't update
- Search bar doesn't respond

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check browser console for JavaScript errors
4. Try a different browser
5. Disable browser extensions

#### Issue: Phone Numbers Not Clickable

**Symptoms:**
- Phone numbers don't open dialer
- Clicking does nothing
- No visual feedback

**Solutions:**
1. On mobile: Ensure device has phone capability
2. On desktop: Phone numbers can be copied manually
3. Check if pop-up blocker is interfering
4. Try right-clicking and selecting "Copy"

#### Issue: Table Not Sorting

**Symptoms:**
- Clicking column headers doesn't sort
- No visual indicators appear
- Sort order doesn't change

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check browser console for errors
4. Try a different browser
5. Ensure JavaScript is enabled

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is this platform free to use?**
A: Yes, the platform is completely free to use. No registration or payment required.

**Q: How often is the data updated?**
A: Data is updated periodically through automated scraping. The frequency depends on when new data becomes available from the source.

**Q: Can I trust the phone numbers?**
A: Phone numbers are extracted directly from the official AFHC website contact modals. However, we recommend verifying contact information before making important decisions.

**Q: What if I find incorrect information?**
A: Please report any incorrect information to the support team. We'll verify and update the data as needed.

### Search Questions

**Q: Why don't I see results when I search?**
A: Make sure you're not filtering too specifically. Try a broader search term or clear the search to see all providers.

**Q: Can I search for multiple criteria at once?**
A: Yes, you can combine search terms (e.g., "Centralia Medicaid" to find Medicaid providers in Centralia).

**Q: Does the search work with partial words?**
A: Yes, the search uses partial matching. You can type "Arian" to find "Arianna Care".

### Data Questions

**Q: Why do some providers show "N/A" or "Not specified"?**
A: Some information may not be available from the source website. We display "N/A" or "Not specified" when data is unavailable.

**Q: How many providers are in the database?**
A: Currently, there are over 580 verified providers in the database, with more being added regularly.

**Q: Are all providers active?**
A: The database includes both active and potentially inactive providers. We recommend contacting providers directly to verify their current status.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (version 90+). Internet Explorer is not supported.

**Q: Does it work on mobile devices?**
A: Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No installation required. The platform runs entirely in your web browser.

**Q: Is my data being tracked?**
A: No, the platform does not collect or track personal user data. All provider data is publicly available information.
