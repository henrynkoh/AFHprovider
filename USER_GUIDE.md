## Step 1: Access the Platform

**Action**: Open your web browser and navigate to the AFH Provider Matching platform URL.

**Details**:
- Works on any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No registration or login required
- Platform loads automatically with all provider data

**What You'll See**: 
- Hero section with platform title
- Stats dashboard showing total providers, cities, and contact info
- Search bar ready for your first search

**Pro Tip**: Bookmark the platform for quick access in the future.

## Step 2: Understand the Dashboard Statistics

**Action**: Review the three statistics cards at the top of the page.

**What Each Stat Means**:
- **Total Providers**: Shows the complete number of verified providers in the database (currently 580+)
- **Cities Covered**: Displays how many unique cities have providers listed
- **With Contact Info**: Indicates how many providers have direct phone numbers available

**Why It Matters**: These stats give you immediate context about the scope and quality of available data.

**Pro Tip**: These numbers update automatically as the database grows.

## Step 3: Perform Your First Basic Search

**Action**: Click in the search bar and type a simple search term like "Centralia".

**What Happens**:
- Results update in real-time as you type
- No need to press Enter or click a button
- Table below shows matching providers instantly

**Try These First Searches**:
- Type "Centralia" to see all providers in Centralia
- Type "Medicaid" to find Medicaid-accepting providers
- Type "LLC" to see all Limited Liability Companies

**Pro Tip**: Start with broad searches to see all available options before narrowing down.

## Step 4: Use Quick Filter Buttons

**Action**: Click one of the quick filter buttons below the search bar.

**Available Filters**:
- **"Centralia"** - Instantly shows all Centralia providers
- **"Medicaid"** - Shows all providers accepting Medicaid
- **"LLC"** - Displays all Limited Liability Companies

**What Happens**: 
- Search bar automatically populates with the filter term
- Results update immediately
- Saves time compared to typing

**Pro Tip**: Combine quick filters with manual search terms for even more specific results.

## Step 5: Understand Provider Table Columns

**Action**: Review each column in the provider table to understand what information is displayed.

**Column Breakdown**:
1. **Area/City**: Location of the provider (e.g., "Centralia, WA")
2. **Name of Business**: Official business name
3. **Name of Provider**: Individual provider or owner name
4. **Phone Number**: Direct contact number (clickable on mobile)
5. **Website**: Provider website or AFHC listing link
6. **Year Started**: When the business was established
7. **Resident Beds**: Number of beds available
8. **Payment Ratio**: Private Pay vs Medicaid breakdown
9. **Others**: Additional information and notes

**Pro Tip**: Hover over rows to highlight them and make reading easier.

---

## Step 6: Sort Providers by Different Criteria

**Action**: Click on any column header to sort the results.

**Sortable Columns**:
- **Area/City**: Alphabetical by location
- **Name of Business**: Alphabetical by business name
- **Name of Provider**: Alphabetical by provider name
- **Year Started**: Numerical by establishment year
- **Resident Beds**: Numerical by bed capacity

**How It Works**:
- First click: Sorts ascending (A-Z or 0-9)
- Second click: Sorts descending (Z-A or 9-0)
- Visual indicator (‚ñ≤ or ‚ñº) shows sort direction

**Pro Tip**: Sort by "Year Started" to find the most experienced providers, or by "Resident Beds" to find larger facilities.

---

## Step 7: Search by Phone Number

**Action**: Type a phone number (full or partial) in the search bar.

**Examples**:
- Type "360" to find all providers with 360 area code
- Type "360807" to find providers with numbers starting with those digits
- Type full 10-digit number for exact match

**Use Cases**:
- You have a phone number but can't remember the business name
- You want to find all providers in a specific area code
- You're verifying if a number belongs to a provider in the database

**Pro Tip**: Phone numbers are stored without formatting, so search for digits only.

---

## Step 8: Combine Multiple Search Terms

**Action**: Type multiple words in the search bar separated by spaces.

**Examples**:
- "Centralia Medicaid" - Finds Medicaid providers in Centralia
- "LLC 360" - Finds LLC providers with 360 area code
- "Care Home Centralia" - Finds care homes in Centralia

**How It Works**: 
- Platform searches for providers matching ALL terms
- Order doesn't matter
- Results show only providers that match all criteria

**Pro Tip**: Start with location, then add payment type or business type for precise results.

---

## Step 9: Use Advanced Filtering Strategies

**Action**: Combine search terms with sorting to create powerful filtering combinations.

**Strategy 1 - Location + Payment Type**:
1. Search "Centralia Medicaid"
2. Sort by "Resident Beds" to see largest facilities first
3. Review providers with highest bed capacity

**Strategy 2 - Business Type + Experience**:
1. Search "LLC"
2. Sort by "Year Started" descending
3. Find newest LLC businesses

**Strategy 3 - Specialty Search**:
1. Search for specialty terms like "Dementia", "Developmental", or "Mental Health"
2. Review "Others" column for detailed specialty information
3. Sort by location to find nearest providers

**Pro Tip**: Use multiple strategies to see providers from different angles and find the best matches.

## Step 10: Contact Providers Directly

**Action**: Click on a phone number or website link in the provider table.

**On Mobile Devices**:
- Tap phone number to open dialer automatically
- Number is pre-filled and ready to call
- No need to manually dial or copy

**On Desktop Computers**:
- Right-click phone number to copy
- Paste into your phone app or VoIP service
- Click website links to open in new tab

**Best Practices**:
- Verify phone numbers are current before important calls
- Check websites for additional contact methods
- Note provider names for reference during calls

**Pro Tip**: Use click-to-call on mobile for instant connection without typing numbers.
