## Email Marketing Best Practices

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly
- Create urgency or curiosity
- Personalize when possible

**Content:**
- Keep main message above the fold
- Use clear, scannable formatting
- Include clear CTAs
- Mobile-optimized design

**Timing:**
- Send welcome emails immediately
- Feature announcements: Tuesday-Thursday
- Reminders: Monday or Friday
- Updates: As needed

**Word Count**: Each email template is 400-600 words (3x longer than typical marketing emails)  
**Format**: HTML email templates ready for email marketing platforms  
**Design**: Responsive, mobile-friendly, professional

---

**Email Platform Integration**:
- **Mailchimp**: Ready for import
- **Constant Contact**: Compatible format
- **SendGrid**: HTML templates included
